The Stance

⟠

The Self as a Site, Not a Soul

You are not a fixed entity. You are contested territory—a site where biology, culture, and trauma have built their structures. Autonomy begins not with self-acceptance, but with a forensic audit of what was built there before you arrived.

⎔

Anti-Advice as Demolition

Conventional wisdom is often the maintenance manual for your own cage. We work with counter-intuition and productive heresy. The goal is not to be contrarian, but to identify and remove the load-bearing lies.

⌬

Sovereignty Over Harmony

The aim is not to fit in, or even to be happy. It is to become sovereign—to have a mind whose weather is its own. This often means embracing friction, enduring alienation, and trading the warmth of the herd for the clear, cold air of self-possession.
