The Core Premise

We have moved from an Attention Economy to an Addiction Economy.
The goal is no longer to capture your attention briefly, but to engineer compulsive loops that extract your time and cognitive capacity as predictably as a resource. Individual discipline cannot win against systemic design. You need a better system.

Who This Is For


The builder who feels their days dissolving into streams. The thinker whose depth is fractured by notifications. Anyone who has tried a "digital detox" only to slide back. This is not about quitting the modern world, but about performing a hostile takeover of your own cognitive environment.
